Frequently asked questions

Are you curious about something? Do you have some kind of problem with our products?

Please feel free to contact us, our customer service center is working for you 24/7.

1. Do I need to create an online account to place an order?

You can shop as a guest for nonprescription items. On the Register/Login page, click on the Continuing as a Guest link. We will collect shipping, billing and contact information for purposes of placing your order, but we will not save this information you will need to reenter it next time you place an order.

2. How do I transfer a prescription from another pharmacy to UPMC Pharmacy?

To transfer a prescription, sign in to your account and click on Pharmacy. Then click on the Transfer Prescriptions button. The next page will ask you to enter your name, date of birth, phone number, current pharmacy name and phone number, and the prescription information. You will have the option of transferring a number of prescriptions or just one.We will do the rest to process your transfer request.

3. What is your return policy for nonprescription purchases?

Our hassle-free return policy allows you to return products to UPMC Pharmacy store or by mail within 30 days of receipt, subject to a few exceptions. Store Returns: Bring any unopened, damaged, or defective nonprescription item(s) along with your receipt (invoice) to any UPMC Pharmacy store to get a credit of the full purchase price. Mail Returns: Call our Customer Care Specialists at 1-(888) 888-8888 to return items by mail. We will work with you to ensure that both your return and credit refund are processed accurately.
